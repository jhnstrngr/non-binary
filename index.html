Typically missunderstood for birth sex. Gender are the expectations society has set on the sexes. Often viewed as stereotypes, that things like clothes, hobbies, sports and professions are given. Think about examples such as makeup, dresses, rugby and nurses which are typically associated with either men or women.
If a trained AI doesn't find it as simple as true or false to figure out what someone's gender is, then maybe gender is a little bit more complicated than a lot of people think.
